Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Clozone AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered sales training platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, company name, job title
  • Profile Data: Display name, preferences, organization details
  • Payment Information: Billing address and payment method (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Communications: Messages you send to us, support requests, feedback

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Training Call Data: Voice recordings, transcripts, call duration, timestamps
  • Performance Data: Grades, scores, analytics, progress metrics
  • Usage Data: Features used, session frequency, login times
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session cookies, analytics cookies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Service Provision: Operating and maintaining the platform
  • AI Training: Improving our AI models using anonymized, aggregated data
  • Performance Analysis: Generating training reports and analytics
  • Customer Support: Responding to your requests and troubleshooting issues
  • Communication: Sending updates, notifications, and marketing (with consent)
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting legal and regulatory requirements

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (Vercel, Render), database (Supabase), AI services (OpenAI, Anthropic, VAPI)
  • Team Members: Within your organization, administrators can view team performance data
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share information

5. Data Retention

We retain your information as follows:

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Canceled Accounts: Data retained for 30 days after cancellation, then permanently deleted
  • Call Recordings: Retained for the duration of your subscription plus 30 days
  • Aggregated Analytics: Anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for AI improvement
  • Legal Requirements: Data may be retained longer if required by law

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Secure authentication and authorization
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Access controls and role-based permissions
  • Data backup and disaster recovery procedures

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
